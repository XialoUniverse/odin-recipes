Egg Scramble

Description

This recipe teaches you how to make a tasty egg scramble with veggies, a simple and quick recipe suitable for any time!

Ingredients

  • 3 Eggs
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • 1 Bell Pepper
  • 1 Small Onion
  • Cooking oil or Butter
  • Optoinal:
    • Salt and Pepper
    • Green Peppers

Steps

  1. Preparing the Vegetables:
    • Wash the tomatoes, bell pepper, and green peppers.
    • Dice the tomatoes into small pieces.
    • Remove the seeds from the bell pepper and green peppers, then chop them into small cubes
  2. Whisk the Eggs:
    • In a mixing bowl, crack the 3 eggs and whisk them together until well combined. You can season them with a pinch of salt and pepper at this stage for added flavor.
  3. Cook the Vegetables:
    • Heat a skillet over medium heat. Add a small amount of cooking oil or butter to the pan to prevent sticking.
    • Add the chopped bell pepper and green pepper to the skillet. Sauté for about 2–3 minutes until they begin to soften.
    • Add the diced tomatoes to the skillet and cook for another 2 minutes until everything is heated through.
  4. Add the Eggs:
    • Pour the whisked eggs over the sautéed vegetables in the skillet. Allow it to sit for a few seconds until the edges begin to set.
    • Gently stir the mixture with a spatula, scraping the cooked eggs from the edges of the pan towards the center.
  5. Scramble the Eggs:
    • Continue stirring gently until the eggs are mostly cooked but still slightly runny. This should take about 3–4 minutes.
    • Remove the skillet from heat. The residual heat will finish cooking the eggs.
  6. You're Done! Serve the dish and add seasonings to your taste.
